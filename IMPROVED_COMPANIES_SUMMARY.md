# KMATS Website - Improved Companies Section Summary

## 🎯 Problem Solved
**Issue**: Business logos were too small and didn't look good in the Companies section.

**Solution**: Completely redesigned the Companies section with significantly larger, more prominent logos while maintaining the sleek dark theme aesthetic.

## ✨ Major Improvements Made

### 📏 **Logo Size Enhancement**
- **Increased logo height**: From 64px to **160px** (2.5x larger)
- **Expanded logo width**: Up to **200px** maximum for better visibility
- **Larger containers**: Minimum **320px** height for each company card
- **Better proportions**: Logos now take up appropriate visual space

### 🎨 **Enhanced Visual Design**
- **Improved glass morphism**: Enhanced backdrop blur and transparency effects
- **Better contrast**: Stronger background overlays on hover for logo clarity
- **Professional spacing**: Increased padding and margins for better visual hierarchy
- **Enhanced hover effects**: More dramatic lift (15px) and scale (1.03x) animations

### 🏗️ **Layout Improvements**
- **Responsive grid**: Optimized for 1 column (mobile), 2 columns (tablet), 3 columns (desktop)
- **Larger gaps**: Increased spacing between cards (12px base, 16px large screens)
- **Better typography**: Larger company names (2xl) and improved descriptions
- **Enhanced section header**: Larger icons and improved spacing

### 🎯 **User Experience Enhancements**
- **Better visibility**: Each logo is now clearly visible and prominent
- **Professional appearance**: Companies section now looks more credible and impressive
- **Improved accessibility**: Better contrast and larger interactive areas
- **Smooth animations**: Enhanced entrance and hover animations
