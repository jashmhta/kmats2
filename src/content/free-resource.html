A short, opinionated checklist for teams building AI assistants, EdTech tools, dashboards, automations, and software products that need to ship without theatre.

01

Define the decision

Write the exact user decision your AI or software product should make easier. One sentence.

02

Map the data

List every input, owner, risk, permission, and source of truth before you design screens.

03

Prototype the workflow

Sequence actions by user intent, not by internal teams or technology layers.

04

Design the trust layer

Add explanations, review states, confidence cues, and recovery paths where users need control.

05

Test small screens

Validate the moments that matter on mobile before the interface becomes too dense.

06

Lock IP and launch rules

Clarify ownership, open-source usage, reusable components, analytics, and confidentiality.

07

Set a measurement plan

Pick one north-star metric and three guardrails before launch — not after.

08

Plan the 30-day list

The improvement list belongs to launch day, not week six. Ship it with the product.
