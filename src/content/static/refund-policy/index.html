Refund Policy

KMATS treats client funds with the same operating discipline we apply to client work. This policy describes how refunds are handled across sprints, projects, and partnership engagements.

Talk to KMATS → Privacy policy

1. Scope

This policy applies to engagements between clients and KMATS AI & Tech Consultancy LLP, including sprints, project SOWs, retainers, and partnership tracks. It governs refundability of advance payments and milestone fees.

2. Sprint engagements

Sprints (2–4 weeks) are paid up-front. If KMATS has not commenced billable work, the engagement is fully refundable. Once discovery has begun, refunds are pro-rated based on hours and artefacts already delivered.

3. Project engagements

Project engagements operate on milestone-based invoicing. Each milestone fee is non-refundable once accepted and signed off by the client. Future milestones can be paused or cancelled with 14 days’ notice and a written reason.

4. Partnership track

Partnership engagements operate at-cost with KMATS holding equity. Cost recoveries are non-refundable but are capped to the agreed at-cost figures. Equity arrangements are governed by the partnership agreement, not this policy.

5. Third-party costs

Third-party costs (hosting, AI providers, plugins, fonts, stock media) are billed at-cost and are non-refundable once paid to the provider. KMATS does not mark up third-party costs.

6. Disputes

If you believe a refund is owed, write to krishankshah@kmats.in with the engagement reference, the disputed amount, and a short summary. KMATS will reply within five business days.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect new engagement models. The version live at the time of contract signature governs that engagement.
