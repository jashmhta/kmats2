Our process

From unclear brief to launch-ready system in five disciplined steps.

The process is built so each step has a tangible artifact you can review, share with your team, and reuse after the project.

01

Define the product goal

Clarify users, use case, data inputs, business model, and the metric that proves the system is working.

02

Map the user experience

Sequence the real workflow, AI touchpoints, screens, edge states, and integration points end-to-end.

03

Design with build constraints

Design with implementation in mind — components, content states, motion, dark mode, and responsive logic.

04

Build, integrate, harden

Engineer the system, integrate AI/automation, and harden against the real production constraints.

05

Launch & iterate

Ship, instrument, and improve. Every project leaves with a measurement plan and a 30-day improvement list.
