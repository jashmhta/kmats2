Who fits at KMATS

People who want to ship product, not produce slides. People who can hold three conversations at once — strategy, design, and engineering — and stay specific in all three. People who care about the smallest viewport, the slowest network, and the user with the least patience.

Open lanes

  • Product UX engineer — design + build, comfortable in React/Vite and component systems.
  • AI workflow engineer — comfortable with LLMs, retrieval, and bounded-AI design.
  • EdTech product designer — strong on cohort UX, content systems, and educator workflows.
  • Full-stack engineer — React/Node/Postgres comfort and a bias to ship.
  • Brand & web designer — opinionated on type, layout, motion, and conversion.

How to pitch yourself

Skip the cover letter. Send a short email with three things you have shipped, what you owned in each, and what you would want to ship next. Links beat PDFs.

Working model

Remote-first, async-friendly, output-based.

Compensation

Project + retainer mix. Partners can earn project equity.

Time zones

Asia, EU, US — daily overlap windows expected.
